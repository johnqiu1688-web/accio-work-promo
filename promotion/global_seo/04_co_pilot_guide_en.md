# Building Your AI-Powered E-commerce Co-pilot with Accio Work: Step-by-Step

In 2026, the most successful entrepreneurs aren't managers of people; they are managers of **AI Agents**. Here’s how to build your first autonomous e-commerce team using Accio Work.

## Step 1: Install Your Command Center
Accio Work isn't a cloud-based web app. It’s a local power-house.
1. Download the Desktop App for Windows or macOS.
2. Install the **Accio Browser Relay** in Chrome (ensure Developer Mode is active).
*Note: This local-first setup ensures your store credentials never leave your machine.*

## Step 2: Configure Your Core Skills
Out of the box, Accio is a generic AI. You need to give it specialized skills. 
*   **For Sourcing**: Enable \`1688_sourcing\`.
*   **For Sales**: Connect your Shopify store via the \`shopify-builder\` skill.
*   **For Marketing**: Enable social media connectors for FB/TikTok.

## Step 3: Run Your First "Agentic Workflow"
Don't just ask the AI a question. Give it a **Goal**.
*   **Bad Prompt**: "Find me some cool products."
*   **Good Prompt (Workflow)**: "Scan the Top 10 trending items in the 'Home Decor' category on Amazon. Find their 1688 counterparts with a price under $15. Draft 5 SEO-optimized Shopify listings for the winners and save them as drafts."

## Step 4: The Human-in-the-Loop Review
Accio will perform the heavy lifting and pause for your approval. Review the supplier choices and the AI-generated copy. Click **Allow**, and watch the automation push the data to your live store.
