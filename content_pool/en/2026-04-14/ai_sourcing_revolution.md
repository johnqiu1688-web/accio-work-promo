## The Traditional Sourcing Bottleneck
For years, scaling a Shopify or Amazon store meant relying on human sourcing agents. These intermediaries provided a vital service, but they came with inherent limitations: 24-hour latency, variable quality control, and a 5-10% commission that ate into already thin margins. 

In 2026, that model is dead.

## AI-First Sourcing: The New Standard
The integration of 1688’s AI-API has unleashed a new breed of "Sourcing Scripts." These aren't just automation tools; they are autonomous decision-makers. Platforms like **Doba Pilot** and **Auto1688** now handle the entire lifecycle of a product—from price negotiation to quality control.

## Scaling to 8 Figures with Zero Human Friction
Successful retailers are no longer building teams; they are deploying agent fleets.
